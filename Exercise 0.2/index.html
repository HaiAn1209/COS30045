Why energy matters

In Australia, household electricity use is strongly influenced by heating/cooling, hot water, and everyday appliances. Choosing efficient appliances and using them wisely can reduce running costs and emissions.

  • Compare energy labels and model specs before buying.
  • Prefer higher-efficiency models when usage is frequent.
  • Reduce standby power where possible (smart power boards help).

Standby power (placeholder)

Many TVs consume a small amount of electricity on standby to support quick-start, updates, or voice features. Over a year, standby use can add up.

  • Disable “quick start” if you don’t need it.
  • Use a smart power strip when devices are idle for long periods.
  • Check manufacturer specs for standby wattage.
